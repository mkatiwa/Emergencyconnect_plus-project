Emergency Connect Plus System

Welcome

The Emergency Response website is designed to enhance and improve emergency services, focusing on healthcare-related emergencies. The system aims to provide quick access to emergency services and enable users to share their location, improving the overall response time and potentially saving lives. The system will give users a registration space to sign up and create a profile, giving them an easy time during an emergency to report the case. The purpose is to provide quick rescue to the emergency victims or else improve emergency services. This has been a great challenge in Kenya, where victims lose their lives due to unattended or late attendance. This system will solve the problem by providing quick two-way communication or alerts between the emergency victim and the respondents.
